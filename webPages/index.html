Heritage

A Medieval Legacy

Finham Brook once fed the defensive 'Great Mere' of Kenilworth Castle. After the Civil War in 1650, the mere was drained, leaving the lush valley we walk today.

Nature

Ecological Sanctuary

The brook is a vital corridor for Kingfishers and Otters. We monitor invasive species like Himalayan Balsam to protect our native flora.

Safety

Flood Mitigation

Finham Brook is prone to flash flooding. We implement "Leaky Dams" upstream to slow the flow and protect the town center.

⚠️ Never attempt to drive through the Ford when water levels are high.
